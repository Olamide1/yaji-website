Our Story

Yaji Lisbon was founded by two sisters, Debby and Judy, who moved to Lisbon and saw a need for authentic Nigerian cuisine. Inspired by the Yaji chili flakes used to spice Suya skewers, they decided to bring the vibrant flavors of Nigeria to Portugal.

Growing up with their grandmother's secret spice blend, Debby and Judy cherished the unique flavors of their heritage. In Lisbon, they missed these tastes and wanted to share them with others. Thus, Yaji Lisbon was born, quickly gaining popularity for its delicious dishes and warm service.
